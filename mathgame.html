Math Game

How To Play

Once the game has started a question will appear, type your answer in and press the ENTER key to see if you were correct! The more questions you get right the higher your score! Questions include addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Fractions are not accepted as answers, please round your answers to the first decimal place.

History

The earliest evidence of written mathematics dates back to the ancient Sumerians, who built the earliest civilization in Mesopotamia. They developed a complex system of metrology from 3000 BC.
