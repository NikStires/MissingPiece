Missing Piece is a class group project created by Nik Stires, Connor Mackenzie, and Darren Jonany. Our goal was and is to create a sleek site where you can play a few simple puzzle games. We wanted to create the opposite of what game sites we remembered, like CoolMathGames, were. The New York Times game page is a good example of the kind of style that we were trying to achieve with this site. While we definitely will not end up with a completely finished product with a diverse cast of games to pick from, we hope that this site meets our initial goals of simplicity and ease of use.
