History

The history of Sudoku dates back to an 18th Century Swiss mathematician’s game called “Latin Squares”. But the modern game of Sudoku as we recognize it today was invented by Howard Garns, a freelance puzzle inventor from Indiana, USA in 1979 when it was published in Dell Pencil Puzzles and Word Games magazine. The puzzle was known as “Number Place,” since it involved placing individual numbers into empty spots on a 9 x 9 grid.
